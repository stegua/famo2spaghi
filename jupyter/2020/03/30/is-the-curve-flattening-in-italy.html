About

This notebook explains how to fit the Italian open-data about the spread of #covid19 using a Susceptible-Infectious-Recovered (SIR) model.

Data fitting is modeled and solved using Constrained Optimization via Pyomo and IPOPT.

Important: I am neither a medical doctor nor an epidemiologist. This post is only about a didactic example of fitting open-data via Mathematical Optimization.
